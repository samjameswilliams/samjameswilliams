# 14

>$^{20}$Brothers, do not be children in your thinking. Be infants in evil, but in your thinking be mature.  

When we do evil, we become progressively more proficient in it. It feels natural to do it and we become mature sinners. If we do not think about God's teaching we remain children in our thinking. We should avoid evil and turn away from the evil we do. We should desire to get out of practice and rusty at sinning but to grow up in doing good.
